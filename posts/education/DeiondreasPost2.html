Want Some Knowledge? Free Resources to Learn a Variety of Topics

  • Deiondrea Berry
  • 20 April 2020
  • 2
  • 117
Share Tweet Pin Email

Covid Cleared Your Schedule? Pick Up Some Knowledge Here

After scouring the internet for free platforms that teach important and accurate information, I came across these sites. I have seperated them by topic so you can just find what you're looking for easily.

  • Finance

    • - Investopedia - This site focuses on investing your money. It'll teach you about the stock market and other ways of investing your money. It is a good resources. If you like their platform you can purchase classes from their Academy feature to learn more.

    • - Nerd Wallet - This site covers all of your finance needs. Their different sections include Credit Cards, Investing, Insurance, Student Loans, Mortgage, Personal Loan, and Savings. Pick a topic and explore from there.

    • - Khan Academy - The content here is limited, but if you are someone who needs videos and thorough explanations, this'll work for you.

  • Coding

    • - FreeCodeCamp - Truly an all-in-one site to handle all of your coding needs.

    • - Harvard via EdX - HarvardX's Computer Science for Web Programming has two sections with the first being an Introduction to Computer Science. A great course to teach you the complete basics and help you apply them.

    • - GeeksforGeeks - A great resource for an overview of different topics. It's like an encyclopedia for coding concepts. It sometimes gives small projects inbetween lessons with solutions and walkthroughs for the solutions.

    • - Use sites like HackerRank ,HackerEarth and LeetCode to practice and apply learned concepts in order to become industry ready.

  • Languages

    • - Duolingo Duolingo had a phone and website to learn with to accomodate different needs. It is by far the most popular probably due to the variety of languages it offers.

    • - Babbel - This is an alternative to duolingo. Maybe theres something you dont like with duolingo. If so, be sure to try out their competition.

    • - Open Culture - Open Culture is similar to the other sites but it also allows for downloading MP3 lessons. Great for the plane trip.
