About Us

The resource made to educate, uplift, and inspire the black community.

Our story

For the 2020 BlueMovement Bootcamp through IBM, black students across the nation from varying univerisites and education backgrounds were given the opportunity to respond to the following prompt:

The Black community in America has been under attack for over 400+ years. From unjust violence to systematic oppression to environmental health bias, our communities have constantly sought equity, safety, and fair treatment within our nation. We are now in a position to stand and demand what we want: Solution-oriented change.

As developers working behind a screen it can prove difficult to contribute to our personal causes in ways that we see fit. This year’s 2020 Blue Movement group project focus will be all about using our development skills to create innovative technological solutions that will directly impact the greater movement of Black American Protection under the Law, Safety, and Mental Wellbeing. With this focus in mind, your team has the freedom to create any technological innovation that they see fit. Be creative and intentional about the purpose of your group’s work.

Our Vision

In response to the project description, The Blacknificent Team has decided to create a resource that provides key information that will educate, uplift, and inspire the black community. We have strived to fullfill our mission in the form of a social media platform called blackVISION that focuses on networking between current HBCU students and alumni.

This is a site that was made for us, by us.

The Blacknificent Team
